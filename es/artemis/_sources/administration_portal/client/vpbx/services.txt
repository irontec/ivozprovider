.. _client_services:

########
Services
########

.. danger:: Services defined in this section **are not accessible during a
   conversation**. They are activated by **calling the codes**, not using
   DTMF codes while talking.

Each client can *customize* the default values assigned by the *brand operator*
using the section **Client configuration** > **Services** and changing the codes
listed there.

.. hint:: Services deleted by the *client admin* will not available to users.
