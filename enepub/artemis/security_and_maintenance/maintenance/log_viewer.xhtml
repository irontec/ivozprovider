Log viewer

Although all production IvozProvider installations maintained by Irontec [https://www.irontec.com] include a ELK stack [https://www.elastic.co/elk-stack], journalctl [https://www.freedesktop.org/software/systemd/man/journalctl.html] is currently the unique tool shipped with IvozProvider to inspect logs generated by different elements of the solution in the past.
