Log viewer

Although all production IvozProvider installations mantained by Irontec [https://www.irontec.com] include a Graylog server [https://www.graylog.org/], journalctl [https://www.freedesktop.org/software/systemd/man/journalctl.html] is currently the unique tool to inspect logs generated by different elements of the solution in the past.
