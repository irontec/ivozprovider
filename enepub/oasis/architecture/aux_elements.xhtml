Aux profile runs software that, even though is not vital for calls placing, makes IvozProvider mantainer’s life much more easier.

In fact, without them, debugging problems would be much harder and the quality of given service would be damaged.

IvozProvider ships:

  • Homer SIP capture: This amazing software lets us capture all the SIP traffic for later analysis, for obtaining statistics, call quality measuring, etc. Visit SIP Capture website [http://sipcapture.org/] for more information.
  • Graylog log viewer: All logs of all IvozProvider profiles are stored and shown with Graylog [https://www.graylog.org/] and divied in brands.
  • Grafana graph dashboard: Grafana [http://grafana.org/] lets us graph everything. Literally.
